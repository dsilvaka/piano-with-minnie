Her approach

Thoughtful lessons shaped around each child.

Her lessons are thoughtfully tailored to each child’s personality, pace, and goals, helping young musicians build strong foundations while developing a genuine love for music.

Minnie works with children ages 4 and above and offers private in-home piano lessons in Manhattan and Queens, including preparation for ABRSM and Trinity examinations. Lessons can be taught in English or Thai. Families need a piano at home so students can build consistent practice habits between lessons.

Her teaching style combines patience, structure, creativity, and encouragement to help every student feel supported and inspired.

Whether your child is just beginning or continuing their musical journey, Minnie’s goal is to make piano lessons joyful, engaging, and confidence-building.
