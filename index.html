Meet Minnie

A beloved piano teacher parents can trust.

Minnie is the experienced teacher families work with directly. With 20+ years of experience teaching children, she brings a calm, motivating presence to each lesson and helps students feel seen, capable, and excited to learn in English or Thai.

Her private in-home piano lessons are available in Manhattan and Queens for families who have a piano at home. Lessons combine patient guidance with clear goals, helping each child build technique, confidence, discipline, and joy in music, from first notes at the piano to ABRSM and Trinity examination preparation.
