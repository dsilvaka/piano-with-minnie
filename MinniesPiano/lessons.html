FAQ

Questions parents often ask.

What age should my child start piano?

Many children can begin piano around age 4, depending on attention span, curiosity, and readiness. Minnie adapts lessons for young beginners.

Do we need a piano at home?

Yes. In-home piano lessons require a piano at home for the lesson itself and for consistent practice between lessons.

Do you offer intro lessons?

Yes. Families can ask about a paid intro lesson to see whether the teaching style, schedule, and lesson format are a good fit.

How long are lessons?

Lesson length depends on the child’s age, level, and goals. Little kids may start with 30-minute lessons, while many students take 45-minute or 60-minute lessons as they grow.

Can lessons be taught in Thai?

Yes. Minnie can teach in English or Thai.

Do you prepare students for ABRSM or Trinity exams?

Yes. Minnie has experience preparing children for both ABRSM and Trinity piano examinations.

What areas do you serve?

Minnie currently teaches in Manhattan and Queens only. Please share your neighborhood to ask about availability.
